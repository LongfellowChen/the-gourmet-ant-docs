隱私權政策

最後更新日期：2026年1月28日

歡迎使用「好鼻師 (The Gourmet Ant)」（以下簡稱「本應用程式」）。我們非常重視您的隱私，本政策旨在說明我們如何收集、使用及保護您的個人資訊。

1. 資訊收集與使用

本應用程式主要提供基於地理位置的餐廳搜尋與偵測服務。為了提供完整功能，我們可能會收集以下資訊：

  • 地理位置資訊：為了使用「美食雷達」功能，本應用程式需要在背景模式下存取您的精確位置資訊，以便在您靠近餐廳時發送通知。
  • 個人收藏與評論：您在應用程式內標記的收藏店家、評論及評分資訊將儲存於您的裝置本地或關聯的雲端儲存中。
  • 裝置資訊：我們可能會收集基本的裝置型號、作業系統版本，用於優化 App 效能與錯誤診斷。

2. 資訊使用目的

  • 提供並維護餐廳搜尋與地圖服務。
  • 在背景自動偵測附近的熱門餐廳並發送通知。
  • 儲存並管理您的個人美食清單與評分。

3. 第三方服務供應商

本應用程式使用了以下第三方服務，這些服務可能會收集用於識別您身份的資訊：

  • Google Maps / Google Places API：用於提供地圖顯示及餐廳資料庫搜尋。

4. 資料保護

我們採取適當的安全措施來保護您的資訊免受未經授權的存取、修改或洩露。您的個人收藏與評論數據主要儲存於本地裝置。

5. 使用者權利

您可以隨時透過裝置的設定介面關閉定位存取權限。請注意，關閉定位權限將導致雷達功能無法運作。

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Welcome to "The Gourmet Ant" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We value your privacy and this policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection and Use

The App primarily provides location-based restaurant search and detection services. To provide full functionality, we may collect the following information:

  • Location Information: To use the "Food Radar" feature, the App needs to access your precise location information in the background to send notifications when you are near restaurants.
  • Favorites and Reviews: Restaurants you mark as favorites, your reviews, and ratings are stored locally on your device or in associated cloud storage.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device models and OS versions for performance optimization and debugging.

2. Purpose of Use

  • To provide and maintain restaurant search and map services.
  • To automatically detect nearby popular restaurants in the background and send notifications.
  • To store and manage your personal food lists and ratings.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

The App utilizes the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Maps / Google Places API: Used for map display and restaurant database search.

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure. Your data is primarily stored on your local device.

5. User Rights

You can disable location access permissions at any time through your device settings. Please note that disabling location permissions will cause the radar feature to stop working.
